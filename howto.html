How to Install a GPU: 7900XTX

Step 1: Prepare Your Workspace

Ensure your workspace is clean and static-free. Gather all necessary tools, including a screwdriver.

Step 2: Open Your Case

Remove the side panel of your PC case to access the interior.

Step 3: Remove the Old GPU

If you have an existing GPU, unscrew and gently remove it from the PCIe slot.

Step 4: Insert the New GPU

Align the 7900XTX with the PCIe slot and gently press it into place until it clicks.

Step 5: Secure the GPU

Use screws to secure the GPU to the case. Ensure it is firmly in place.

Step 6: Connect Power Cables

Connect the necessary power cables from your PSU to the GPU.

How to Install a CPU: Ryzen 7 9800X3D

Step 1: Prepare Your Workspace

Ensure your workspace is clean and static-free. Gather all necessary tools, including a screwdriver.

Step 2: Open Your Case

Remove the side panel of your PC case to access the interior.

Step 3: Remove the Old CPU

If you have an existing CPU, release the retention arm and gently remove it from the socket.

Step 4: Insert the New CPU

Align the Ryzen 7 9800X3D with the socket and gently place it into position. Lower the retention arm to secure it.

Step 5: Apply Thermal Paste

Apply a small amount of thermal paste to the top of the CPU.

Step 6: Install the CPU Cooler

Attach the CPU cooler according to the manufacturer's instructions. Ensure it is firmly in place.
